How It Works

Every participant enters the arena as an individual — no teams, no alliances... yet.

  • Each player receives a Circle, Star or Square
  • These groups will compete in different challenges — some physical, some mental, and some… unexpected.
  • After initial rounds, groups will be split and re-formed into Teams, testing loyalty and adaptability.
  • Only those who rise through the ranks — and survive eliminations — will face the final stage.
